3. Two Numbers That Tell the Whole Story

A. HAMILTONIAN SHARE OF DEBT
"How much of our borrowing is actually building America?"

Definition: Share of federal borrowing that funds hard assets:

  • Energy (oil, gas, nuclear, grid, refineries)
  • Factories & chip fabs
  • Ports, rail, highways, bridges
  • Water systems & critical minerals
  • Defense capital (ships, missiles, drones)
Today: ≈ 18%
"Only 18 cents of every borrowed dollar builds anything. The rest is already spent."
Trump Target: 30%+
"Under Trump, we turn bad debt into good debt. We borrow to build America."
B. BUILD RATE – Public Investment as % of GDP
"How fast are we actually building?"

Definition: Federal investment in hard assets as a share of the economy.

History:

  • 3-5% – WWII, Interstates, Apollo (America at full power)
  • ≈ 2% today – Coasting while China builds
Today: ≈ 2%
Trump Target: 3-5% Build Rate
"From a 2% decline rate to a 4-5% Build Rate. That's how you make America great again – physically."

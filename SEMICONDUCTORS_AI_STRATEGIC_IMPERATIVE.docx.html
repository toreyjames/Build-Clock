Critical Insight (The “So What”)

1:1 is a system goal, not a fab goal. If we only talk design/manufacturing, we miss the upstream “characters” (grid, UPW, chemicals/gases, wafers, rare earth magnets, packaging) that decide whether 2030 is achievable.

Heartbeat AI is the glue. It increases throughput and reliability across every upstream dependency — not just inside the fab.
